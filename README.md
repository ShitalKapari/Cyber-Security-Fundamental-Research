# Cyber-Security-Fundamental-Research
This project presents a comprehensive cybersecurity analysis of the 2011 Sony PlayStation Network (PSN) cyberattack, one of the largest data breaches in the gaming industry. It examines the attack through the lens of Advanced Persistent Threat (APT) techniques, explores the impact on user data, business operations, and organizational reputation, and reviews relevant national and international cybersecurity regulations. The project also includes risk assessment, prevention and mitigation strategies, incident response planning, and recommendations based on industry best practices from the perspective of a Chief Information Security Officer (CISO).
